About Me

Hi there! My name is Menno. I am a PhD-TA candidate at the FSA group at the Technical University of Eindhoven (TU/e).

Currently my research is about investigating how symmetry reduction can play a role in the theory of model checking on which mCRL2 is based.

Topics that are of interest to me include Automata Theory, Category Theory, Testing Techniques, Model Checking, Symmetry Reduction, Equation Systems.
